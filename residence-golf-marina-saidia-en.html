The environment

A gated, secluded and peaceful residence

The residence is gated and secured, with a panoramic golf view and a pool. The surroundings are deliberately secluded: scattered homes, undeveloped plots between residences, a paved road with a pavement. That calm is what makes the difference — and it comes with an honest trade-off: a car is required.
