Who we are

A local team, two complementary trades

Azur Stay serves two audiences in Saïdia. On one side, travellers who book a holiday apartment directly, with no Airbnb or Booking commission, a real human contact and confirmation within minutes. On the other, owners — often Moroccans living abroad — who entrust us with the rental management of their property.

With 8 years of experience in the Oriental region, we apply hotel-grade standards to every stay: properties verified and prepared by us, quality linen, careful welcome and concierge available 7 days a week, in French and Arabic. Our on-the-ground presence makes all the difference — a real person, never an automated line.

Our territory

Saïdia, the Blue Pearl of the Mediterranean

With its 14 km of fine sandy beach, lively marina and generous sunshine, Saïdia is one of north-east Morocco's most loved seaside destinations. Our properties sit in the heart of Quartier Franco and at the Marina, minutes from the beach and the marina, and about 25 minutes from Oujda-Angad airport.

Based in Saïdia today, we are gradually extending our presence across the region: Saïdia, Oujda, Nador and Berkane. The ambition stays the same everywhere — a local, human and reliable service.
