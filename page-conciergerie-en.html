Frequently asked questions from owners

What does the concierge service include?

Listing creation and distribution, pricing, guest check-in, cleaning, linen, maintenance and rental income reporting.

What are your management fees?

A transparent commission on the income generated, with no hidden fees. Personalised quote on WhatsApp.

I live abroad — how does it work?

This is our core expertise with Moroccans abroad: our local team handles everything on site, and you track your income remotely. Everything is arranged via WhatsApp, in French or Arabic.

Can I keep personal use of my property?

Of course. You tell us the dates you'd like to keep for yourself, and we block them on the calendar.

Is long-term winter rental guaranteed for my apartment, and what are the conditions?

We deploy a Google SEO strategy unique to Saïdia to capture monthly tenants. To join this programme, your property must meet our winter-comfort criteria: working reversible air conditioning, dedicated high-speed Wi-Fi and complete equipment (washing machine, fitted kitchen). For your financial peace of mind, if no monthly request comes in for a given month, your calendar automatically stays active for nightly stays on the international platforms. With Azur Stay, you multiply your opportunities with no risk of a blocked calendar.
