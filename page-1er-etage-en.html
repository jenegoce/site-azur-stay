Description

A spacious 110 m² family apartment on the first floor, with independent access — a single apartment per floor — in a quiet residence in Saïdia town centre, less than 700 m from the beach. It comfortably sleeps 4 adults and 2 children.

The air-conditioned master bedroom has a large double bed; the second bedroom (not air-conditioned) offers 2 twin beds and a large closet; the lounge completes the sleeping arrangements with 4 sofa beds. Large air-conditioned lounge, satellite TV and free Wi-Fi; modern bathroom with walk-in shower and hydromassage system; fully equipped kitchen; laundry room with washing machine, iron and ironing board.

Upstairs, a 70 m² rooftop — built-in barbecue, lounge corner and dining area — is shared with Darcom Rooftop and Darcom Loft. Free public parking nearby. Book direct for the best price, with the cleaning fee waived. The Azur Stay concierge team is on site, 24/7.
