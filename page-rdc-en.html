Description

On the ground floor, with fully autonomous step-free access — not a single step — Darcom Garden offers 90 m² and total privacy, in a quiet residence in Saïdia town centre, less than 700 m from the beach. Ideal for 2 adults and 2 to 3 children. No air conditioning: the apartment stays naturally cool.

Its 12 m² private courtyard (4 × 3 m) is perfect for breakfast, tea or a relaxing moment. The master bedroom has a large double bed and a large built-in wardrobe; the large Moroccan lounge (4 benches) sleeps 2 to 3 children. Satellite TV and free Wi-Fi; large modern bathroom with walk-in shower; separate equipped kitchen with washing machine.

Free public parking nearby. Book direct for the best price, with the cleaning fee waived. The Azur Stay concierge team is on site, 24/7.
