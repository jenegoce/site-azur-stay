The neighbourhood

Quartier Franco: a quiet neighbourhood, close to everything

Quartier Franco is part of the Dallas area in Saïdia. It's a residential, quiet neighbourhood, mostly home to Moroccans living abroad (MRE): very calm outside the season, livelier in summer. Here are the walking distances from our apartments.
